Cross-selling Recommendations for Banking Products

As a Data Analyst for a Spanish bank, I analyzed a dataset of 13,647,308 customer records to gain insights into customer behavior and identify cross-selling opportunities for banking products. Using Python and various libraries, I cleaned and visualized the data. I standardized column names, handled missing values, and created a dataframe with 949,609 unique customers. For further analysis, I exported the sorted dataframe to a CSV file and imported it into Power BI. Within Power Query, I refined the dataset, and grouped customers by age and income. Using Data Analysis Expressions (DAX), I calculated total products owned by each customer. From the analysis, I discovered cross-selling opportunities: marketing debit cards to current account owners and promoting pension accounts to payroll account owners. I recommended focusing marketing efforts on high-represented age and income groups, streamlining products, and enhancing customer experience. Implementing these recommendations will drive business growth and increase revenue.
Tools Used: Python, Power BI
